Workshop author guide

It will be updated as additional information becomes available, the updates will be announced by email.

1. Camera-ready paper, deadline 3 April 2025 AoE

  1. Publish the code and data, if applicable.
  2. De-anonymize: your paper (including author names, acknowledgements, and external links) and supplementary materials.
  3. Ensure correct formatting and template. Some decisions came conditional on fixing formatting issues. We are not sure whether they can be truly "conditional", i. e. whether it's possible to change the acceptance decisions at this stage, and, to be honest, we don't want to find that out. Spotlight spots we definitely can. If yours has this comment, please do a service to our audience, and fix the formatting.
  4. Camera-ready papers do not have a strict page limit, but keep in mind that readers like crisp and to-the-point writing.

2. Promotion

If you make a social media post announcing your paper, feel free to tag us, we will repost: LinkedIn, X, Bluesky.

3. Presentation formats

Accept (Oral): 15 minutes talk, not including questions. Slide format is 16:9. You can present from your laptop, or ours, in the latter case the slides must be in pdf. You can optionally display a poster as well.

Accept (Poster): Per official ICLR rules, the maximum size allowed is 61cm x 91cm (24in x 36in) width/height, portrait format. We the organizers guarantee that there is enough space for every conforming poster. If you bring a non-conforming poster, we won't reject it outright, but you assume the following risk: should the room run out of space, we will take down the non-conforming posters starting from the largest and least aesthetically pleasant. You will have an opportunity to give a 1 minute spotlight talk as well. Given the time constraints of the format, we will assemble all slides into a single presentation. To be included, you must upload your single 16:9 slide in pdf format to the corresponding "Spotlight" entry in the iclr.cc workshop agenda before 09:00 Singapore time April 26th.

iclr.cc Please make sure the information in the iclr.cc workshop agenda is correct, update if necessary.

Remote participation: The workshop will be streamed via iclr.cc. If you are unable to attend in person, we can put up your poster in the room. To enable this, please order it via the official ICLR printing service and contact Mengyi Chen. We can also arrange for you to upload the recording to SlidesLive, for this contact Nikita Kazeev.

4. Panels

The workshop will feature two panels with keynote, contributed (Oral) & contributed (best Poster) speakers, you can submit questions in advance here or ask on the spot.

5. Workgroup sessions

Interactive and collaborative discussion session at the end of the workshop. We will have exchanged diverse ideas on multiscale problems, approaches and methods. How can they be made into concrete actionable projects?

6. Collaboration pitches

There will be an opportunity for authors to pitch the jobs and concrete collaboration opportunities within the scope of the workshop. If you are interested, please fill the form before 09:00 Singapore time April 26th.

7. Workshop logos

The use is not mandatory, but we would be happy to see them in your presentations and posters.
